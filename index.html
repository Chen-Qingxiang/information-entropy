A fair coin is maximally uncertain, so its entropy is 1 bit. A deterministic coin has entropy 0 because the next toss is predictable.

Notes and Interpretation

          

        


        

          

            
What entropy says

            
    
              
  • Entropy measures average uncertainty or average information per symbol.
    • 
              
  • With log base 2, the unit is bits.
    • 
              
  • It is about probability distributions, not labels or meanings.
    • 
              
  • It is central to communication, coding, compression, and noise.
    • 
            

          

          

            
Common confusions

            
    
              
  • High entropy does not mean important.
    • 
              
  • Low entropy does not mean bad.
    • 
              
  • Randomness and complexity are related, but not identical.
    • 
              
  • A random string can be hard to compress and still carry no useful message.
    • 
              
  • A meaningful sentence can contain redundancy and have lower entropy.
    •
